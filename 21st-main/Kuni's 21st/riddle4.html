Riddles (Japanese Occupation)

Answer: A Kerosene lamp
Explanation: A kerosene lamp has black soot but was used for light during power shortages in wartime.

Answer: A radio
Explanation: The radio was a primary source of news and entertainment during the occupation.

Answer: A banana
Explanation: A banana holds many fruit pieces inside its peel, resembling a house with no doors.

Answer: A lice
Explanation: Lice were common during wartime; people searched for them but immediately got rid of them when found.

Answer: A gun
Explanation: A gun is powerful, and when fired, it releases deadly bullets like fire.
