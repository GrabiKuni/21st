Riddles (Pre-Colonial)

Answer: A Cricket
Explanation: Crickets are nocturnal and chirp at night, hence “singing” in the dark.

Answer: Sweat or Tears
Explanation: These are forms of water produced by the body that cannot be bought.

Answer: A guava tree
Explanation: The guava fruit has small, hard seeds that resemble stones.

Answer: A lizard
Explanation: A lizard’s tail can appear sharp and pointed, resembling a nail.

Answer: A mango
Explanation: The skin of a mango is like a protective covering, similar to how clothes cover the body.
