Riddles (Spanish Colonial Period)

Answer: A chair
Explanation: A chair has a body (the seat) and legs (feet), but no head or shoes.

Answer: A tree
Explanation: A tree is living, but it is neither a person nor an animal.

Answer: A moon
Explanation: The moon is round like a ball, but it is too far away to be physically touched or hit.

Answer: A shadow
Explanation: A shadow appears when there is light (during the day) but disappears when it’s dark (at night).

Answer: A bones
Explanation: Bones are the framework of the body, but they do not have a soul.
