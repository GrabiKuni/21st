Riddles (American Colonial Period)

Answer: Kettle
Explanation: A kettle has a "stomach" or body to hold water but no soul.

Answer: Rabbit
Explanation: A rabbit lives in the mountains but doesn’t plant crops.

Answer: Key and lock
Explanation: A key and lock are often paired together, and both have “tails” in the form of their ends.

Answer: A clock
Explanation: A clock’s hands move like walking, but it has no feet.

Answer: A clothesline
Explanation: A clothesline "wears" different clothes hung to dry.
